Budgeting Basics

Your guide to effective money management

What is Budgeting?

Budgeting is the process of creating a plan to spend your money. This plan is called a budget. It helps you prioritize your spending and save for future goals.

Steps to Create a Budget

  1. Determine your income
  2. Track your expenses
  3. Set your financial goals
  4. Create a budget plan
  5. Review and adjust regularly
